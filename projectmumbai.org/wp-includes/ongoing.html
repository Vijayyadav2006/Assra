Assra Welfare empowers citizens and creates a culture of positive action.

True philanthropy is not just about pledging wealth—it’s about investing time, skills, and effort to create lasting impact. Volunteerism lies at the heart of meaningful change, and Assra exemplifies this by mobilizing citizens to take ownership of their city’s challenges.Assra Welfare has proven that collective action can transform Mumbai into a kinder, more resilient city.

Assra Welfare has stepped in beautifully to fill a critical gap in well-executed public-private-people partnerships, addressing the challenges faced by millions daily. During the pandemic, their rapid response and deep community engagement made a tangible impact, and their continued work towards an inclusive and sustainable Mumbai is truly inspiring. We are proud to have partnered with Assra Welfare and look forward to seeing them grow from strength to strength.

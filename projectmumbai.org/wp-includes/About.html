We Are On A Mission To Empower Communities And Help The Helpless.

OUR MISSION

As a Model of Public-Private-People partnership,

Aasara Welfare has a vision of making Mumbai—and the extended Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR)—a better place to live, work, play with every citizen volunteering to do his/her/their bit for the community as we at Aasara Welfare, playing the role of a catalyst—ensuring social transformation through initiatives of scale.

Collaborative initiatives through Innovation, Integrity, and Transparency, where People remain our Purpose and Positive change to their lives, our intended Objective.

OUR GOAL

Aasara Welfare's goal is to strive to build such public-private-people partnership models ensuring social transformation through initiatives of scale across as many cities as possible.

Clean Water

Our mission is to provide long lasting solutions to make clean water accessible for everyone everywhere.

Mental Health

We provide help in terms of mental health benefits to people across Maharashtra, especially those in extreme need.

Volunteering

We are always in need of fresh volunteers. We aim to get as many as possible for smooth program operations.

Food

We aim to provide healthy and nutritious meals to all so that no one ever sleeps hungry.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

Making urban spaces accessible to the differently-abled, elderly, and neurodiverse through inclusive infrastructure.
